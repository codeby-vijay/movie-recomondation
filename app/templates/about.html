About CineAI

An AI-powered movie recommendation system built with Machine Learning, Flask, and the MovieLens dataset.

🎯

Content-Based Filtering

Uses TF-IDF vectorization and cosine similarity to recommend movies based on genres, tags, and metadata similarity.

👥

Collaborative Filtering

Leverages KNNBasic and SVD algorithms from the Surprise library to find patterns in user behavior and predict ratings.

🔀

Hybrid Approach

Combines content-based and collaborative filtering with weighted scoring for the most accurate recommendations.
